// THE LAW
Rules of the Syndicate
RULE 01
🎭 THE SETUP
  • Each lobby holds 6 to 10 souls. Roles are dealt at random and kept secret.
  • One third of the lobby are Traitors. They alone know each other.
  • The rest are Loyalists. Some carry hidden gifts: Oracle, Medium, Arbiter.
  • Your role is shown only to you. Reveal it at your peril.
RULE 02
☀️ THE DAY
  • The day opens with open discussion. Speak, accuse, defend, mislead.
  • Every message you send is logged. Words become evidence.
  • Mentioning a player by codename raises their Heat. Watch who the room is converging on.
  • Any living player may Call the Vote early to skip discussion.
  • When the timer runs out, the vote begins automatically.
RULE 03
⚖️ THE VOTE
  • Each living player casts one vote, or abstains.
  • The player with the most votes is banished and their role is revealed.
  • Ties are broken by the Arbiter if one is alive. Otherwise no one dies.
  • The condemned receive a 5-second Last Words window — one final accusation before the silence.
  • Lying in the vote is not just allowed — it is expected.
RULE 04
🌑 THE NIGHT
  • When dusk falls, the city sleeps. The Traitors do not.
  • Traitors choose one player to eliminate. Their identity is found at dawn.
  • The Oracle may peek at one soul's true alignment — once per game.
  • The Medium hears whispers from the recently dead, often naming a real Traitor.
  • If no Traitors choose a target, the night is silent. This is rare.
RULE 05
🏆 THE VICTORY
  • Loyalists win when every Traitor lies banished or slain.
  • Traitors win the moment they equal or outnumber the remaining Loyalists.
  • Banished Loyalists count as a Traitor victory point. Choose your votes carefully.
  • The dead see all at game's end. The full table is revealed in the final reckoning.
RULE 06
⚔️ THE CODE
  • Deception is the weapon, not the crime. Lie freely.
  • The dead may watch but cannot vote, chat, or act.
  • Do not name your role unless you mean to die for it.
  • Quiet players draw suspicion. So do loud ones.
  • Trust no peek. Trust no whisper. Trust no vote. Trust no one.
The Syndicate does not punish lies. It punishes the gullible who believe them. — FOUNDING DOCTRINE, YEAR ZERO
